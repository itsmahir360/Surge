Skill

  • Main Attack - Power Surge: Fires a long-range energy blast that splits upon impact, making him lethal around walls.
  • Super - Self-Upgrade!: The core of his gameplay. Using his Super permanently evolves him to a new stage, enhancing his abilities.
    • Stage 1 Upgrade: His main attack splits into three projectiles instead of two for superior area control.
    • Stage 2 Upgrade: Replaces his Super with Serve Ice Cold, a quick teleport for dodging, engaging, and jumping over walls.
    • Stage 3 Upgrade: His main attack projectiles now ricochet to additional targets, allowing him to dominate grouped enemies.
